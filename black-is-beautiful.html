The Ambler NAACP

The mission of the Ambler NAACP is to secure the political, educational, social, and economic equality of rights in order to eliminate race-based discrimination and ensure the health and well-being of all persons.

We serve the North Penn, Wissahickon, and Upper Dublin school districts.
